Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I file my Income Tax Return (ITR) in Chas, Bokaro?

You can visit our branch office at Main Road, Chas (near Bihar Colony), Bokaro — 827013, or share your documents on WhatsApp/email and we will file your ITR completely online. Most salaried returns are completed within 1–2 working days.

What documents are needed for ITR filing?

For salaried individuals: PAN, Aadhaar, Form 16, bank statements, and investment/deduction proofs (LIC, PPF, tuition fees, home loan interest, etc.). For business owners: books of accounts or bank statements along with GST data where applicable. We will guide you on the exact list for your case.

Do you handle GST registration and monthly returns in Bokaro and Kolkata?

Yes. We handle fresh GST registration, amendments, monthly/quarterly GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filing, annual returns (GSTR-9/9C), ITC reconciliation, and departmental notices for clients in both Chas–Bokaro and Kolkata, as well as fully online for clients anywhere in India.

Can you prepare CMA data and project reports for bank loans?

Yes, this is one of our core services. We prepare bank-ready CMA data, project reports, and financial projections for working capital and term loans, structured to the standards credit appraisal teams expect.

Do I need to visit the office, or can everything be done online?

Most services — ITR, GST returns, registrations — can be completed fully online. Share documents over WhatsApp or email, and we handle the rest. Office visits at Chas (Bokaro) or Kolkata are welcome whenever a face-to-face discussion helps.
